Executive Profile

Asri Tri Undari

Chief Operating Officer

Asri is the Chief Operating Officer. She graduated from Diponegoro University with a major in Library and Information Science. She earned her first master’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Indonesia and her second master’s degree from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), majoring in Technology and Computer Systems, where she was awarded the Korean Government Scholarship Program (KGSP).

Asri began her career as a researcher at Republika and SWA. She later joined Indonesia Corruption Watch (ICW) as a Public Campaign Officer and also served as a lecturer at Bina Nusantara University.

She is currently a lecturer at the University of Indonesia in the field of Information Technology and is pursuing her doctoral degree in Computer Science at Bogor Agricultural University (IPB).
