Board of Advisors

Selo Winardi

Strategic Advisor

Selo Winardi is an accountant who graduated from the State College of Accountancy (Sekolah Tinggi Akuntansi Negara) in Jakarta in 1985. He earned his MBA from the Institute of Education and Management Development (IPPM) in 1991. Selo also holds a Certified Accountant (CA) designation from the Indonesian Institute of Accountants (Ikatan Akuntan Indonesia) and a Certified Professional Management Accountant (CPMA) designation from the Indonesian Management Accountants Association (IAMI).

Selo is a Commissioner at Sintesa Group. Before joining the company, he worked as an Auditor at the Financial and Development Supervisory Agency (BPKP), PT Trikora Lloyd, and PT Danapaints Indonesia. He is an active member of the Financial and Development Supervisory Agency (BPKP), a member of the Indonesian Management Accountants Association (IAMI), and serves as a board member at the Indonesia Services Dialogue.
