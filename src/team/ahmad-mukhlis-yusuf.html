Board of Advisors

Ahmad Mukhlis Yusuf

Strategic Advisor

Ahmad Mukhlis Yusuf is an Executive Coach and Corporate Strategy Enabler with over 25 years of experience working closely with senior executives across consulting, business leadership, and organizational transformation. His career reflects a strong balance of strategic leadership and practical execution at the highest institutional levels.

He previously served as CEO of ANTARA News Agency and Chairman of the Organization of Asia‑Pacific News Agencies. He has also contributed to organizations such as Impac, Bina Nusantara University, and the Tazkia Group. He is currently serving as Independent Commissioner at PT Bogor Life Science and Technology.

Ahmad holds a Bachelor’s degree in Agricultural Social Economics from the Institut Pertanian Bogor, a Master’s degree in Strategic Management from the Asian Institute of Management, and a Doctorate in Marketing Strategy from the University of Indonesia.

Known for his ability to enable strategic clarity at the top level, Ahmad contributes advisory direction that strengthens leadership systems, communication, and organizational performance for sustainable transformation.
