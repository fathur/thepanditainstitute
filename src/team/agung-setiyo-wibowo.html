Executive Profile

Agung Setiyo Wibowo

Founder & CEO, The Pandita Institute

Agung is Founder and CEO of the Pandita Institute. He brings 13 years of experience in Marketing Communications, Consulting, and People Development. He has served in various well-known institutions, including The ASEAN Secretariat, Partnership for Governance Reform, Schlumberger, SWA, AndrewTani & Co., Panin Dai-ichi Life, and Jafra.

His journey began early — publishing his first book at the age of 19 — and has since evolved into writing, ghostwriting, and co-authoring more than 100 books and over 1,000 articles across business, leadership, HR, management, and related fields.

Agung’s work and perspectives have been featured in more than 70 national and international media outlets, including The Japan Times, SEA Today, Kompas, Jawa Pos, RRI, Detik.com, Kumparan, Media Indonesia, Liputan 6, Pikiran Rakyat, Tribunnews.com, Smart FM, Sonora, Suara Merdeka, Merdeka.com, Koran Jakarta, TVRI Jakarta, Batam Pos, Tanjungpinang Pos, Harian Bernas, KapanLagi.com, Jawa Pos TV, Kompas.id, and many others.

He has spoken in Jakarta, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Bangkok, Manila, New Delhi, and Dubai, delivering programs that integrate storytelling, leadership, and brand strategy — guiding more than 100,000 individuals, from executives to entrepreneurs and from professionals to public figures, to unlock their potential, sharpen their message, and amplify their influence.

Agung graduated from Paramadina University with a major in International Relations, where he was the awardee of the Paramadina–Theodore Permadi Rachmat Fellowship. He earned his master’s degree from the University of Indonesia on a full scholarship from the Ministry of Higher Education, and completed a non-degree program in International Humanitarian Law & Human Rights at Singapore Management University.

Born a natural public speaker and storyteller, he is dedicated to helping people grow and glow. In his spare time, he enjoys gardening, traveling, and jogging.
